Onboarding Curriculum

A self-paced path for new members — from the command line to molecular simulation, machine-learned force fields, and the habits of good research.

Each module lists an estimated time: a rough guide for a newcomer working through it part-time, not a deadline. Area 1 tooling modules ≈10–20 h · Area 2 domain modules ≈30–50 h · Area 3 research-skills modules ≈10–25 h — spread them out and revisit as needed.
